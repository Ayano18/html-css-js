Women with Baskets

by Anita Magsaysay-Ho

About the Artist

Anita Magsaysay-Ho (1914–2012) was a Filipino painter and the only female member of the "Thirteen Moderns," a group of artists who helped reform Philippine art. She is best known for her stylized portrayals of Filipina women engaged in daily tasks like harvesting, weaving, and selling fish. Her work often features rhythmic compositions, soft lighting, and a deep appreciation for the labor and spirit of rural women.
